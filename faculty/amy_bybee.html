Amy Bybee has been involved in music and the Suzuki community since she was 5 years old. She studied the violin in Salt Lake City with Doralee Madden and Kory Katseanes. During her high school years, she studied violin in Nagoya, Japan with Ms. Noriko Kitagaki.

Amy has taught violin and currently teaches Music and Movement classes for preschoolers in Henderson, NV. She enjoys hiking, reading, and spending time with her husband and 6 children.
