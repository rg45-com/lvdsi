Mrs. Laura Thiele is a registered Suzuki Piano teacher with thirty years of musical directing, classroom teaching, and private studio teaching experience. In addition to training in Suzuki Piano Pedagogy, Mrs. Thiele holds certifications in the Orff and Kodaly music methodologies and is a licensed Musikgarten teacher and Early Childhood Music Specialist.

Originally from Arizona, "Miss Laura" (as she is known to her students) moved to Henderson, Nevada and opened the Green Valley Piano Studio in 2015. In addition to music, she enjoys hiking, biking and spending family time with her three grown children and two grand-daughters.
