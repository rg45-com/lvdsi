Las Vegas Desert Suzuki Institute is a non-profit organization dedicated to enhancing children's music education. We host biannual music camps for kids and rely on donations to further our goal of delivering quality youth music education - we greatly appreciate your donation!

Las Vegas Desert Suzuki Institute

Welcome to the 2018 Las Vegas Desert Suzuki Institute! We invite you to join us as we celebrate our ninth year as a quality music camp. We welcome all students of Flute, Piano, Violin and Viola as well as teachers seeking to obtain Suzuki book registration. Students ages 3-6 may join us in their very own "Intro to Music" 1/2-day camp. The LVDSI student institute runs July 17-19, 2018. Enjoy exploring our website and we look forward to seeing you in July.
