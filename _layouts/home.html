Las Vegas Desert Suzuki Institute is a non-profit organization dedicated to enhancing children's music education by offering quality programs in the Las Vegas area. We host summer workshops as well as Suzuki events throughout the year to further our goal of delivering quality youth music education.

The 2019 Las Vegas Desert Suzuki Institute will be enjoying its tenth year in Las Vegas, Nevada. We are thrilled to offer our Suzuki flute, violin, viola and piano students as well as siblings, and their parents this musical experience. Intro To Music classes for ages 3-8 and Beginning Flute and Violin 1/2 day camps are also available. Teacher Training for ECC, and Flute Units 1 and 2 are our teacher courses.

LVDSI features Repertoire, Technique, Ensemble and Master classes as well as more in-depth work for our advanced Flute students Books 5 and up. Please check our website for more detailed information on our enrichment and core classes for students.

Performance opportunities include: Informal Noontime Student Performances, Ensemble Extravaganza and Final Concert. We are excited to sponsor a Sonata Competition with the winners performing on the Wednesday noon program.
