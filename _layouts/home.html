Play Music. Explore Creativity. Discover Potential.

An innovative week of musical development for students of all ages.

Las Vegas Desert Suzuki Institute is a non-profit organization dedicated to enhancing children's music education by offering quality programs in the Las Vegas area. We host summer workshops as well as Suzuki events throughout the year to further our goal of delivering quality youth music education.

We welcome all students who play flute and piano and preschool aged children, and we invite you to join us as we celebrate our ninth year as a quality music camp, offering masterclasses, group workshops, ensemble classes and more. Students ages 3-6 may join us in their very own "Intro to Music" 1/2-day camp. The LVDSI student institute runs July 17-19, 2018. Enjoy exploring our website and we look forward to seeing you in July.
