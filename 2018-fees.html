Intro to Music

  • Tuition $75
  • Registration fee if paid by 4/1/18 will be $25
  • Registration fee if paid between 4/2/18 & 5/15/18 will be $50
  • Registration fee if paid between 5/16/18 & 7/15/18 will be $75

Our Intro to Music Camp is a half-day camp for children ages 3-6 who have either not yet begun formal music study or have only been playing a short while. Children attend each day from 9am-12noon and receive a General Music Class, a Carnival of the Animals (Introduction to the Instruments) Class, and a Vocal Explorations Class. We do limit each class to 8 students.

These young students are invited to bring lunch to eat at noon with the rest of the students. Intro to Music Classes will perform on Thursday afternoon at 12:45 immediately following lunch.

Please note: A parent or guardian is required to attend with their child.

Flute Program

  • Tuition $350
  • Registration fee if paid by 4/1/18 will be $50
  • Registration fee if paid between 4/2/18 & 5/15/18 will be $75
  • Registration fee if paid between 5/16/18 & 7/15/18 will be $100

All flute students will receive a daily Master Class, Technique Class, Repertoire Class, Flute Ensemble (placed by level), Performance Workshop and an Enrichment Class of their choice. Flutists who own a piccolo, alto or bass flute are encouraged to bring them to LVDSI for both Technique and Ensemble classes.

All Flute Ensembles and Repertoire Classes will be performing on the Thursday Evening Final Concert. Individual parts for all ensembles will be assigned and emailed upon completed registration.

Registered flute students may audition for the Tuesday afternoon Honors Recital and we encourage students in Books 3 and up to audition for this year's Sonata Competition. Please see competition details under the "Students" tab.

Piano Program

  • Tuition $350
  • Registration fee if paid by 4/1/18 will be $50
  • Registration fee if paid between 4/2/18 & 5/15/18 will be $75
  • Registration fee if paid between 5/16/18 & 7/15/18 will be $100

The Piano Program is open to all Suzuki Piano students in Books 1-4, as well as non-Suzuki trained students with equivalent playing abilities. Students will attend a daily Master Class, Technique Class (Books 1&2) or Accompanying (Books 3+), Piano Ensemble, Performance Workshop, Repertoire Class and an Enrichment Class of their choice. All Ensemble Music will be assigned and emailed to students upon completed registration. The Ensembles and the Repertoire Classes will perform on the Thursday Evening Final Concert.

Registered piano students may audition for the Tuesday afternoon Honors Recital and we encourage students in Books 3 and up to audition for this year's Sonata Competition. Please see competition details under the "Students" tab.

Sibling Enrichment Classes

$40 per class
