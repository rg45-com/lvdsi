Register

Enrichment Classes are a great way to explore music in a new way!

All students in our Flute or Piano program will select an Enrichment Class as part of their program. First Choice selections are not guaranteed, as classes are filled based on time of enrollment, class availability, and individual schedules. Siblings and Friends are welcome to register for Enrichment Classes also! (additional fees apply)

Vocal Workshop

This class will focus on learning more about the vocal instrument that we all have, and how to use our voices to improve our instrument studies. This will be fun, upbeat and educational class split by age.

Theory Games

Does music theory feel boring or overwhelming? This class will help break down the biggest concepts in music theory by using fun games and twisting and flipping music theory around like you have never seen! Classes will be split by age and experience.

Percussion Ensemble

This class will focus on developing rhythmic understanding through the use of different types of percussion instruments. Students will be reading written rhythms, and may even help to compose some percussion ensemble music! Percussion Ensemble students will perform on the Final Concert on Thursday night.
