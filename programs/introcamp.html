Intro to Music

$75 Tuition + Registration fee

Register

Our Intro to Music Camp is a half-day camp for children ages 3-6 who have either not yet begun formal music training or who have only been studying for a short time. Children attend each day 9:00-12:00 and receive a General Music Class, a Carnival of the Animals (Introduction to the Instruments) Class, and a Vocal Explorations Class.

Please Note: a parent or guardian is required to attend with each child.

Intro to Music Classes will perform on Thursday afternoon at 12:45 immediately following lunch.

Each class is limited to 8 children, and a minimum enrollment must be reached by June 1st to guarantee the classes.
Ages 3-4: Bitty Beethovens
Ages 5-6: Mighty Mozarts

Enrollment Fee: $75 plus a non-refundable Registration Fee
