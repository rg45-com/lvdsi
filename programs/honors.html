The Honors Recital is open to every student who is registered for the LVDSI Workshop. Solo works from the Suzuki Repertoire as well as pieces outside the repertoire for advanced students may be submitted. All video auditions must be played from memory. Piano accompaniment for flute students is optional. Piano entries should be repertoire played hands together. All entries are due no later than June 25, 2018 and winners will be notified by July 1, 2018.

Students must submit a VIDEO recording of a Suzuki repertoire piece. The recording should be from memory, but piano accompaniment is not required. Students should pay attention to the quality of video, sound, and should be dressed for a performance, and their full body should be visible. Please announce your name and the name of your piece at the start of your recording. Send a link to the video (YouTube or otherwise) to the following email address: {{ site.email }}.

Winners will perform on the Tuesday noon recital. Flute students will be notified by July 10 of their assigned rehearsal time with their accompanist.
