LVDSI will not be offering Teacher Training in 2018.

As we are a 3-day workshop vs. a 5-day institute, there are not enough required Suzuki class hours for trainees to observe.

The 2019 LVDSI Institute will be a full, 5-day institute with teacher training offered in both flute and piano as well as ECC. Specific books for Suzuki Registrations will be announced by October 2018.
