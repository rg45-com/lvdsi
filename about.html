About Las Vegas Desert Suzuki Institute

The Board of Directors of the Las Vegas Desert Suzuki Institute is pleased to offer our summer program of exceptional musical classes, enrichments and performance opportunities in beautiful Temple Sinai in Summerlin. For those who wish to support students in need of financial assistance, our non-profit status affords you to make a monetary donation and enjoy both the personal tax benefit as well as the satisfaction of knowing you enhanced a child's musical life.
